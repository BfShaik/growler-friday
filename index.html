FAQ

What’s Growler Friday?
Growler Friday is a celebration that’s kind of like sitting around a campfire with friends - but instead of a fire, there’s a growler! Pop it open, pour one for you buddies, and sit back and relax; it’s as easy as that. Growlers build teamwork and comradery, because you should always have a group of friends to help you take down a whole growler. Oh, and it has to take place on Friday. We spelled it out in case you hadn’t figure that out yet.
What’s a Growler?
A growler is typically a 64 oz vessel that you fill up with fresh beer right from the tap. You can think of them like refillable mini kegs sold by breweries. You’ll have to check your state’s laws, but usually you can only legally fill up a growler on the premises of the brewery that produces that beer. That means you can’t go to your local bar down the road and fill up whatever you want. Growlers can also come in 32 oz sizes.
Are there any rules to using a Growler?
YES. Once you fill up a growler, you should plan to drink it within a week, and the absolute golden rule is that once you open it - you need to drink it all within that session. This is why you probably don’t want to do Growler Friday alone - you’ll want a team helping you. Resealed growlers will lose carbonation quickly, and no one wants that.
What Time Should I Celebrate?
Whenever you feel the need to say goodbye to your work week and say hello to the weekend! Maybe that's during lunch, or maybe that's at a friday night get-together - you decide!
